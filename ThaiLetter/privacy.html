Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for using ThaiLetter (“the App”). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any of your personal information through the App itself.

  • No personal data collection: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

  • No user accounts required: You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to use the App.

3. Information Collected Automatically by Google Play

When you download and use the App through Google Play, Google may automatically collect certain information as part of its standard services. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • App installation events
  • App usage statistics
  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system version)

This information is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy, and we do not have access to or control over this data.

4. Use of Information

Since we do not collect any personal information through the App, we do not use or share any such information for any purpose.

The only data available to us is aggregated, anonymized statistics provided by Google Play Console, which we use solely to understand general app performance and improve the App experience.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any of your personal information with third parties. No user data is collected, so no data is shared.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the App and any information that may be processed through it. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
